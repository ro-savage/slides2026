One platform connecting manufacturers, owners and equipment.
- Embed — Tagged at production: Scannable partners with manufacturers to embed digital identities at production — NFC, datagrids, RFID or QR.
- Manage — Auto-inventoried: Owners track inventory, inspections and compliance automatically. No manual entry, no paper records, no missed inspections.
- Extend — Retrofit anything: Owners can manually tag any non-SmartPPE item to bring it onto the platform.
